Is this a scam? Do you have any evidence it works?

UVC disinfection is not a scam. Many scientific studies, including CDC and EPA studies, prove that UVC light kills almost all biological pollutants and germs. For example, a study published in the Asian Pacific Journal of Tropical Biomedicine named "Effect of germicidal UV-C light(254 nm) on eggs and adult of house dust mites, Dermatophagoides pteronyssinus and Dermatophagoides farinae" showed the efficacy of UVC on killing dust mites and their eggs. The EPA also states that "UV lamps may destroy indoor biological pollutants such as viruses, bacteria, and molds." How is the UVO254™ Lamp different from traditional UVC lamps or Ozone generators?

The UVO254™ Lamp combines both UVC light and Ozone generation in one device, making it more effective than traditional single-function devices. While standard UVC lamps only work through direct light exposure, and traditional ozone generators only use O3, our device provides comprehensive disinfection through both methods simultaneously. Does it work on mold?

Yes, UVC light and ozone are both effective against mold. UVC light disrupts mold DNA, preventing it from reproducing, while ozone oxidizes mold spores and mycotoxins. The EPA confirms that both UVC and ozone are effective treatments for mold remediation when used properly. However, for extensive mold problems, professional remediation may still be necessary.

What happens to allergens and germs after being broken down?

When UV light and ozone break down allergens and germs, they are converted into harmless basic compounds. Proteins are broken into amino acids, complex organic compounds are reduced to simple molecules, and bacteria/viruses are rendered inactive. These harmless byproducts are either filtered out of the air or naturally dissipate. There's no toxic residue - everything is converted to safe, natural components.
